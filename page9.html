Robo-Lab

Robo-lab is a top down 2D facility management simulator based on the Ex-Machina film. The game is played by having the player place rooms on a grid and gradually produce resources in order to construct robots. The player can progress by unlocking more facilities, and more robot types.
The game is won when the God AI is researched and constructed and the game is lost if the facility runs out of power.
